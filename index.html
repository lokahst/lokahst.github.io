Legends of the North

"Expand vanilla Minecraft with an immersive Skyrim-inspired RPG experience featuring leveling, combat skills, enchantments, and scalable mobs."

Project created: July 7, 2026

The plugin isn't story-driven(it wont be), but it includes mechanics inspired by the game mentioned above, such as Health, Stamina, and Magicka. Currently, only Health and Stamina are functional. Magicka is there for now... mostly as decoration.

Key Features

  • Leveling system
  • Custom enchantments
  • Disenchant gear to permamently learn its magical effects
  • Designed to enchance not remove

Why LOTN?

There are many really good MMORPG/RPG plugins that have many features and maybe even years of development. But will LOTN be one of them? One of "really good" ones?

That's good questions and hopefully there is a good answer.

Developing MMORPG/RPG plugins is really hard thing to do because of many mechanics, many parts that are connected to eachother. If one part fail, half of plugin is broken. So that's why it will take a long time to optimize it and make it playable.

So practically.. there isn't answer to that question yet. Because it's new. Not many people know the plugin. And this plugin does not have any special functions that are ahead of others. But let's try the best.
